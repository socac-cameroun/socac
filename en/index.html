About SOCAC

SOCAC is an integrated agribusiness organization combining animal production, crop production, feed supply, operational management, and customer service. Our ambition is to build a modern, credible, and sustainable company able to respond effectively to local market needs and professional partners.

🎯

Our Mission

To produce and supply quality, accessible, and reliable agricultural and livestock products with strong attention to performance, supply consistency, and customer satisfaction.

🌟

Our Vision

To become a leading integrated agribusiness reference in Cameroon and Central Africa through disciplined growth, modern operations, and long-term value creation.

💚

Our Values

Quality, responsibility, reliability, customer closeness, and continuous improvement shape our decisions across the company.

IFMS System

IFMS (Integrated Farm Management System) digitizes and manages our operations: feed mill, stocks, farms, purchasing, health control, reporting, and dashboards. It is a key lever for production management, traceability, and better decision-making.

Smart & Integrated Management

IFMS (Integrated Farm Management System) is our proprietary solution that digitizes and optimizes all of our agricultural operations.

  • Complete feed mill management
  • Purchasing and inventory tracking
  • Farm management (pig, poultry, fish)
  • Health and nutrition control
  • Orchard management
  • Administration and access control
  • Real-time dashboards
  • Automated reports and analytics
