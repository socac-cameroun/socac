Building a modern and sustainable agriculture for Cameroon

The Agro-Food Cooperative Society of Cameroon (SOCAC) promotes modern and sustainable agriculture. It develops livestock and food production activities including pigs, fish farming, broiler chickens and laying hens, contributing to economic growth and food security in Cameroon.
