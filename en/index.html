An agro-food cooperative committed to modern agriculture in Cameroon

The Agro-Food Cooperative Society of Cameroon (SOCAC) promotes modern and sustainable agriculture. It develops livestock and food production activities, including pigs, fish farming and poultry production (broiler chickens and laying hens), while actively contributing to economic development and food security in Cameroon.

About

A strong agricultural vision for Cameroon

SOCAC operates through a modern, structured and sustainable cooperative model focused on production, livestock, value creation and community development.

🎯

Mission

Produce quality food, expand modern livestock farming and contribute sustainably to food security.

🌍

Vision

Become a leading integrated agro-food organization in Cameroon and Central Africa through structured and responsible production.

🤝

Values

Quality, innovation, cooperation, responsibility, sustainability and positive local impact.
